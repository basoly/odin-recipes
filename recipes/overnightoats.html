Easy Overnight Oats

Overnight oats that are very simple to make.

Insert description of easy-to-make overnight oats here.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups of rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup milk of choice
    • ]
  • 1/4 cup of non-fat Greek yogurt.
  • 1 tablespoon of chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon sweetener
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients into a large glass container and mix until combined
  2. Cover the glass container with a lid or plastic wrap. Place in the refrigerator for a least 2 hours or overnight. Toppings can be added the night before or immediately before serving.
  3. Uncover and enjoy frome the glass container the next day. Thin with a little more milk or water, if desired.
