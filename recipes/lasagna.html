Ingredients

  • tomato sauce
  • mozarella cheese
    • ]
  • ricotta cheese
  • Ground beef
  • salt
  • pepper
  • lasagna noodles
  • parsley
  • onion
  • garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. brown beef, onion and garlic over medium high heat in skillet or dutch oven
  3. stir in pasta sauce, add salt and pepper. simmer uncovered in medium heat until thickened.
  4. spread meat sauce on casserole, then top with 3 lasagna noodles.
  5. Layer with ricotta cheese
  6. cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes
  7. remove foil and sprinkle with remaining 2 and half cups mozzarella cheese.
  8. rest for at least 15 mins before serving.
